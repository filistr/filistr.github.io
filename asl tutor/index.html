ASL Alphabet Tutor

Practice your skills in American Sign Language Alphabet with this application. Please read the following instructions before starting.

1. Position your camera against a natural background.

2. Fingerspell the displayed letter within the frame. Ensure only your hand is in the image.

3. Press CHECK to verify if your answer is correct.

Earn 10 points for a correct answer and lose 5 for an incorrect one. The game progresses to the next letter regardless. Incorrectly answered letters will reappear later in the game. You successfully complete the tutor session by correctly fingerspelling each letter at least once.

Tip: Hover over the ellipse below for help with the letters.
