ASL Alphabet Game

In this application, you get to practice your skills in the American Sign Language Alphabet. Please read the game instructions before starting.

1. Start by position your camera in front of a natural background. Do not include anything more in the image but your hand.

2. Fingerspell the displayed letter within the frame.

3. Press the check button to see if your answer were correct.

If you answer correctly, you earn 10 points. If you answer incorrectly, you lose 5 points. Either way, the game moves on to the next letter. However, the incorrect letters will return later in the game. The user can only win the game by answering correct to all letters at least once.

P.S If you need any help during the game, hover over the ellipse below to display all letters.
