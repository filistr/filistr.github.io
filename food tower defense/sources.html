FOOD TOWER DEFENSE

INSTRUCTIONS

Note: Please play with the console CLOSED!

The Food Tower Defense game is a variation of the Bloons Tower Defense Game, with the objective of capturing ingredients.

Players can explore three distinct worlds: desert, forest, and ice. Each world offers unique maps, graphics, and starts with varying amounts of money, towers, and ingredients.

Various types of towers capture different ingredients, as indicated when hovering over a tower in the control panel on the right. The number of ingredients a tower can capture is displayed in parentheses "()".

Players can upgrade or sell towers after purchase. Upgrading a tower increases its target area. Each tower can only be upgraded once.

Strategically drag and drop towers along the path. Note that towers cannot be placed on paths, obstacles, or other towers. The tower's target area, shown as a circle, indicates where it can capture ingredients. This area turns red if a tower is placed in a prohibited location. Players may need to click again to drop the tower in an allowed spot.

Each world features 10 waves of ingredients. A countdown timer is linked to each wave, allowing users to call the wave early for bonus coins. Upcoming ingredients are also displayed on the timer for strategic planning. Each wave introduces new ingredients, towers, or increases ingredient speed.

Have fun and aim to conquer all the worlds!
